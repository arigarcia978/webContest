Lastest Events

  • RNR 2015

    The National Meeting of the IEEE Student Branches is an activity whose mission is to integrate all Student Branches that are within the same country once a year.

    It's an initiative created by the Student Branch IEEE Tucuman in 2013, trying to meet the needs of our partners and ours seeking knowledge and preparation, with different themes to normally addressed in the obligatory subjects of the careers.

  • TISP 2015

    The Teacher In-Service Program (TISP) provides a forum for IEEE volunteers to demonstrate the application of engineering, science and mathematics concepts by sharing their real-world experiences with local pre-university educators. IEEE offers training workshops for its volunteers on how to provide in-service programs to local pre-university educators.
